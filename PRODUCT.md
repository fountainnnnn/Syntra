# Syntra Product Context

register: product

## Product Purpose

Syntra turns Telegram customer-service conversations into live customer operations intelligence. It ingests real Telegram messages, extracts structured business state with OpenAI when configured, falls back deterministically when secrets are absent, and shows leads, tickets, tasks, risks, sentiment, pipeline movement, workload, and recurring issue clusters in a premium light-mode dashboard.

## Core Users

The primary user is a small business owner, operations manager, or support lead who uses Telegram as a customer-service channel. They need to understand what is urgent, which customers are at risk, which leads need follow-up, and what work the team must do next without maintaining a traditional CRM by hand.

## Demo Business

The demo business is ApertureOne Events, an event photography company handling booking inquiries, corporate leads, payment confirmations, delivery delays, refund complaints, package upgrades, and invoice follow-ups through Telegram.

## Product Principles

- Telegram-first: the dashboard should always make it clear that operational state begins with Telegram conversations.
- Dashboard-first: Syntra is not a chatbot wrapper or prompt UI; it is a command center for running customer operations.
- Source-evident AI: every AI conclusion should connect back to a message, conversation, customer, task, or opportunity.
- Demo reliable: real Telegram and OpenAI paths must work when secrets exist, with honest fallback states when they do not.
- Light-mode premium: large readable UI, warm off-white surfaces, strong hierarchy, restrained color, and no dark app shell.

## Tone

Calm, operational, precise, and confident. The product should feel like a serious workbench for a busy owner, not a decorative AI demo. Copy should be short, direct, and action-oriented.

## Anti-References

- Generic CRM clone.
- Chatbot wrapper.
- Streamlit-style demo.
- Dark neon AI SaaS.
- Pure-white admin panel with weak hierarchy.
- Card farm dashboard.
- Purple/blue gradient landing page.
- Mock-only product that claims real Telegram ingestion.

## Success Moment

During a two-minute demo, a judge sends a customer-service message to the Telegram bot, then sees that message become an urgent conversation, extracted intent, task, revenue risk, pipeline movement, and graph path inside Syntra within a few seconds.
